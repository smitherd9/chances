What are the Chances? food poisoning in NYC

What is it?

What are the Chances? is an app designed to give you an idea of your chances of getting food poisoning at restaurants across New York City.

How does it work?

What are the Chances? gets restaurant inspection data from New York City's Open Data API and calculates a rating based on the each restaurant's average inspection score and the average number of times a particular grade was received. It then returns a number from 1 to 10, with 1 being the smallest chance of sickness and 10 being the highest.
